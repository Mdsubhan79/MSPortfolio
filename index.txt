# about

About me

I’m an aspiring Data Analyst, focused on understanding data and turning it into meaningful insights that can guide better decisions. I enjoy working with data, exploring patterns, and simplifying complex information into something clear and useful.

Alongside this, I come from a strong background in full stack development, which helps me think beyond just analysis. I understand how systems are built, how data flows, and how everything connects. I like building things that are not only functional but also purposeful, where both logic and insight come together.
